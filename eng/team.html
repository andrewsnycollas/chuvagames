Meet the Team

"We are a collective of neurodivergent game lovers who got together to tell stories."

Andrews Nycollas

Creative Director / Game Designer


Andrews Nycollas has been passionate about game development since his preteen years, when he began creating and selling custom arcade fighting games at school at the age of 12. He has a diverse background in theater, music, cinema, and visual arts, which enriches his creative approach to game design. A professional freelancer in the game industry since 2012, Andrews has been working extensively with the Asian gaming market since 2019. "I sold everything I had and came to the Philippines to meet a girl, we got married, we have a baby boy. Now I am ready to become an indie game dev."

Marcos Genú

Art Director


Marcos Genú has been working since 2012 in concept art, 2D animation, and asset creation for games. He has contributed to various projects, including Tap Tap Beer and games developed for major companies such as VALE and the Government of Pará. His work also includes internationally awarded games like Ghostein, which was recognized at Game Jam+, and a project that secured sixth place at the global BGJam. "Oi, eu sou o Marcos."
