About Us

CHUVA Games CO


We are a collective of neurodivergent game lovers who came together to tell stories.

Our journey began on March 15, 2024, when Andrews Nycollas, founder of CHUVA Games CO. and leader of our development team, embarked on the solo development of our first authorial project. Recognizing the power of collaboration, Andrews soon invited friends from university and online communities to join him. Together, we are bringing our unique perspectives and passion for gaming to create unforgettable experiences.
