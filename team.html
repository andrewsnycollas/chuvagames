Meet the Team


Andrews Nycollas - Creative Director / Game Designer


Andrews Nycollas has been passionate about game development since his preteen years, when he began creating and selling custom arcade fighting games at school at the age of 12. He has a diverse background in theater, music, cinema, and visual arts, which enriches his creative approach to game design. A professional freelancer in the game industry since 2012, Andrews has been working extensively with the Asian gaming market since 2019. "I sold everything I had and came to the Philippines to meet a girl, we got married, we have a baby boy. Now I am ready to become an indie game dev."

André Borba - Sound Designer / Producer / Community Manager


André was born in 1992 and has been passionate about technology from an early age. His curiosity about how computers work began when he received his first PC, a Pentium III, in the late '90s. His first video game was a NES, followed by the SNES, PS1, PS2, and eventually an Xbox 360. He studied classical piano and keyboard for seven years at SESI, performing in his first recital at age 12. During his teenage years, he shifted from piano to bass and formed his first hardcore band at 17, which lasted until he was 23.
